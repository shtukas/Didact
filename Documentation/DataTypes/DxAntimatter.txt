
DxAntimatter: 
    "uuid"        : String
    "mikuType"    : "DxAntimatter"
    "unixtime"    : Float
    "datetime"    : DateTime Iso 8601 UTC Zulu
    "description" : String
    "familyId"    : String
    "charge"      : Float

DxAntimatters are issued by Cores.
Negative charge means that we owe that time.
We redraw time from the bank at the uuid account to update the charge.
When the charge is positive then, we can merge two such items (one positive and one negative)
