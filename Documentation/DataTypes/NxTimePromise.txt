
NxTimePromise: (Blade)
    "uuid"          : String
    "mikuType"      : "NxTimePromise"
    "unixtime"      : Float
    "account"       : String
    "value"         : Float

A NxTimePromise is a promise of a bank update operation. When the time reaches `unixtime`, the value (algebraic value) is added to the bank account. They were introduced to smooth NxBoard time management after bursts of activity.
